Web version of the Monero (xmr) miner.

Dependencies:
- WebAssembly (http://webassembly.org/)
- SIMD (https://developer.mozilla.org/ru/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/SIMD & https://github.com/ljharb/simd)
- Multicore (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Web_Workers_API & https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/NavigatorConcurrentHardware/hardwareConcurrency)
- GPU (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/WebGL_API & https://github.com/stormcolor/webclgl)
- Crypto (https://developer.mozilla.org/ru/docs/Web/API/Window/crypto & https://github.com/fireice-uk/xmr-aes-encryption.git)
